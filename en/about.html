About us

Our history

From traditional tailoring craft to a modern factory

ILHAM GROUP began as a small tailoring workshop and, over the years, became one of Uzbekistan's leading suit and trousers manufacturers. At every stage, we have stayed true to our commitment to quality.

Today our factory is equipped with modern tailoring machinery, experienced engineers and a skilled team of tailors producing goods for local and international clients.

Our mission

Preserving quality and trust in every stitch

Our mission is to elevate Uzbekistan's tailoring industry to international standards, grow local manufacturing, and deliver the highest quality products to our clients.

Every suit and pair of trousers passes through strict quality control stages — an expression of the respect we hold for our clients.

Manufacturing process

From fabric to finished product

01

Modern and classic models are developed for every season.
